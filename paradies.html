ABOUT OUR RESTAURENT

Paradise is one of the famous Restaurants in serving a Worlds favorite Biryani. Established in the year 1953 at Secunderabad with a small Canteen and cafe in a cinema theater. The food became so popular in the '60s. In 1978, Mr. A Hemati took complete charge of the Restaurant from his Father & Uncle and renovated according to social trends. Paradise expanded its operations in different cities like Bangalore, Chennai, Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, and Gurugram. Selling over 25,000 plates of biryani a day in Hyderbad, having 35 plus outlet food chain has experienced phenomenal growth in the last 11 years.
